For the last 150 years, we have put kindness into action, helping millions of people in the UK an around the world get the support they need when crisis strikes. From hiring a wheelchair or dealing with loneliness, to adjusting to life in a new country - we’re there when you need us.

We are able to combine postage & ship internationally but please contact us for a quote before bidding.
We are not able to ship to a requested alternative address, the desired address must be updated in pay pal / eBay before payment. This is to comply with the Pay pal protection policy.
We aim to dispatch all items within 3 working days

Returns are accepted on orders within 30 days of receipt of the item
Return postage will be paid for by the buyer unless the item is faulty
Items that are Hygiene, beauty or skincare are non-returnable, unless the item is faulty.
International orders returns are accepted within 14 days of receipt of the item, return postage of the item is paid for by the buyer.
